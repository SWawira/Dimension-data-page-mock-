Certified Top Employer since 2009


We are proud to be a certified Top Employer in South Africa since 2009. The Top Employer Institute uses various metrics such as talent strategy, digital on-boarding experience, learning & development opportunities, leadership development, career and succession management plans and compensation and benefits to identify companies that qualify as a Top Employer. Our people are at the heart of what we do. It is our mission to ensure we constantly seek ways to improve the employee experience and remain a proudly certified Top Employer.
